Doom Pile

Doom Pile is a simple tool from Not Built For This that helps adults with ADHD and executive dysfunction sort overwhelming admin into manageable next steps.

In short

In short: Doom Pile is for the moment when your brain will not let you start. Instead of giving you a full productivity system, it helps you get unstuck with one clearer next step.

What Doom Pile helps with

Doom Pile is designed for bills, post, forms, renewals, appointments, inbox dread and the admin tasks that keep repeating in your head because they still are not done.

Why it works

When people feel overwhelmed, the problem usually is not information. It is friction. Too many choices, too much shame, too much uncertainty and no easy way in.

Doom Pile is built to reduce that friction so a task feels smaller, less loaded and more startable.

Frequently asked questions

Doom Pile FAQs

What is Doom Pile?

Doom Pile is a simple tool from Not Built For This that helps adults with ADHD and executive dysfunction sort overwhelming admin into manageable next steps.

Who is Doom Pile for?

It is for adults who get stuck around life admin, paperwork, messages, avoided tasks and the shame that grows around them.

Is Doom Pile only for people with ADHD?

No. It is designed with ADHD and executive dysfunction in mind, but it can help anyone whose brain freezes around admin.

How does Doom Pile help with life admin?

It helps by reducing friction. Instead of making you organise everything, it helps you get to one more manageable next step.

Do I need to be organised to use it?

No. Doom Pile is for the exact moment when being organised feels impossible.

Is this a productivity app?

Not in the usual sense. It is practical support for getting unstuck, not a pressure system.

Can I use Doom Pile if I feel overwhelmed?

Yes. That is when it is most useful.

This is practical support, not medical advice.
