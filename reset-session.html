Frequently asked questions

Reset Session FAQs

What is a Reset Session?

The Reset Session is a guided body-doubling session for adults with ADHD who want to take one small step towards an admin task they have been avoiding.

What happens during the session?

You start with gentle regulation, then move into guided triage and body doubling using the ADHD Admin System.

Do I need to bring a specific admin task?

No. You can bring a task, a pile, an inbox or just the thing that keeps circling in your brain.

Is this therapy?

No. This is practical support, not therapy or medical care.

Is it suitable for adults with ADHD?

Yes. It is designed for adults with ADHD, executive dysfunction and life-admin overwhelm.

What will I leave with?

You should leave with one task made less scary, a simple next step, a weekly reset plan and the 12-page ADHD Admin System workbook.

This is practical support, not medical advice.
