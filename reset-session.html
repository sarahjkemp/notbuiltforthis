Reset Session

The Reset Session is a guided body-doubling session for adults with ADHD who want to take one small step towards an admin task they have been avoiding.

In short

In short: this is the most supported option. If you want calm structure, body doubling and a low-shame way back into an avoided admin task, this is the place to start.

What happens in a Reset Session?

A calm, no-shame online reset for adults with ADHD who feel buried by post, forms, bills, emails and life admin. The session starts with a gentle mobility and breath reset, then moves into guided admin triage and body doubling using the Not Built For This ADHD Admin System.

Body doublingGentle structureOnlineNo shame

What to bring

Bring one thing you have been avoiding: a letter, bill, form, inbox, renewal, appointment task, or just the admin thought that keeps circling your brain. You do not need to be organised. You do not need to have your pile ready. You just need to show up.

What you leave with

One task made less scary

The point is not to fix your whole life in one sitting. It is to reduce the dread around one real thing.

A simple next step

Something concrete you can return to after the session without having to reinvent the plan.

A weekly reset plan and the workbook

You also leave with the 12-page ADHD Admin System workbook so the support does not end when the session does.

What to pair it with

If you want support between sessions, use Doom Pile for a simpler next step or the ADHD Admin System for a self-paced structure.

Frequently asked questions

Reset Session FAQs

What is a Reset Session?

The Reset Session is a guided body-doubling session for adults with ADHD who want to take one small step towards an admin task they have been avoiding.

What happens during the session?

You start with gentle regulation, then move into guided triage and body doubling using the ADHD Admin System.

Do I need to bring a specific admin task?

No. You can bring a task, a pile, an inbox or just the thing that keeps circling in your brain.

Is this therapy?

No. This is practical support, not therapy or medical care.

Is it suitable for adults with ADHD?

Yes. It is designed for adults with ADHD, executive dysfunction and life-admin overwhelm.

What will I leave with?

You should leave with one task made less scary, a simple next step, a weekly reset plan and the 12-page ADHD Admin System workbook.

This is practical support, not medical advice.
