What is body doubling for ADHD admin?

In short

In short: body doubling means doing a task alongside another person so it feels easier to begin and keep going. For ADHD admin, it can reduce avoidance by making the task feel less lonely, less wobbly and less loaded.

What body doubling means

Body doubling is not someone doing the task for you. It is another person sharing the space, attention or structure while you do the task yourself.

Why it works for admin

Admin often becomes harder in isolation. The presence of another person can make a task feel more real, more anchored and less likely to drift.

That is why body doubling can help with paperwork, bills, inboxes, calls and appointments.
