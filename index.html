What Not Built For This Is

Not Built For This creates low-friction tools, guides and sessions for adults with ADHD, executive dysfunction and life-admin overwhelm.

We help people who feel buried by post, forms, bills, renewals, emails, appointments and unfinished tasks build simple systems that reduce shame, lower friction and make life admin feel possible.
