Frequently asked questions

ADHD Admin System FAQs

What is the ADHD Admin System?

The ADHD Admin System is a practical workbook and system for sorting post, forms, bills, renewals, emails, appointments and life-admin tasks without shame.

Who is it for?

It is for adults with ADHD, executive dysfunction and life-admin overwhelm who need a simpler system.

What kind of admin does it help with?

Post, forms, bills, renewals, emails, appointments, calls and everyday avoided admin.

Do I need to complete everything at once?

No. The system is meant to be used in smaller chunks, not one perfect sprint.

Is it designed for executive dysfunction?

Yes. It is built for people whose brains struggle with initiation, sequencing and shame around admin.

How is it different from a normal productivity system?

It is designed to reduce friction, not impose more pressure.

This is practical support, not medical advice.
